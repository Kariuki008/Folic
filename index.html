Rev David T Muriu is married to Mary Wanjiru for the last thirty five years ago. He is father to Neema Wairimu, Winnie Wanja and Rose Wambui who have all finished their university education. He is the pastor of Fountain of Life Churches Int'l Kitengela. Rev DT has been working with the young people both in schools and among the neighborhood and countryside. He has done apostolic ministry among the Masai for over thirty years. As the pioneer of the Gideons International Bibles ministry he lead in distribution of over 100,000 bibles in Narok County. Rev DT Muriu has been in the leadership of Kitengela Pastors Fellowship in the last ten years. He is a trained Building Technology professional and a licensed civil engineering contractor. Rev DT Muriu believes this is the time for Africa and especially Kenya. The Gospel is the Power of God unto Salvation to everyone whatever the background or status. Kenya has got the potential, favor and capacity to bring Revival in Africa. It's a Glorious Revival Moment in Kenya.
